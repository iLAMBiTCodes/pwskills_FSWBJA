A favicon of a website is a small icon of a site or a page that is shown at the address bar or in the browser tab and this icon shows the brand logo of the website.
Here is an illustrative example:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
    <link rel="shortcut icon" href="./music.png" type="image/x-icon">
    <title>Music</title>
</head>
<body>
    <!-- Music content goes here -->
</body>
</html>
